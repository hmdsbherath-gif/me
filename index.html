01 · About

Teaching ICT with purpose

I'm an ICT teacher based in Kandy, Sri Lanka, with over a decade of classroom experience guiding students from their first Scratch block to building real web applications. My approach mixes practical projects, computational thinking, and a healthy dose of curiosity.

Beyond the classroom, I run coding clubs, mentor students for national ICT competitions, and create open learning resources for teachers across the country.
